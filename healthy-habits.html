Help

How this works?
Let us know the meals and calories you would like to eat in a day. We will compute the calories with the recommendation from the health department and divide your expected daily calories into each meal. Then, our system automatically generates the recipes for you!

How did calories and meal calculate?
Two meals: 30% on breakfast and 70% on lunch.
Three meals: 20% on breakfast, 50% on lunch and 30% on dinner.
Four meals: 10% on breakfast, 50% on lunch, 30% on dinner and 10% on snack.

Max calories
An accurate report is located on the right side after you generate the receipt. Limite represents the max calories you should eat per meal. The total represents the calories on our recipes recommended for this meal.
